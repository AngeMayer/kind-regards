I started Kind Regards because I deeply understand the need for strategy, but too often experienced implementation that lacks momentum. Roadmaps launched with ambition, then stalled. Action plans developed, but milestones not achieved. Kind Regards exists to close that gap, moving great ideas into the real world and turning them into impactful results.

A decade of turning ambition into impact.

I am passionate about impact, real impact. With over a decade in corporate and community sustainability, my experience is deeply rooted in environmental and social impact for a circular economy.

I've led complex programs for corporates, governments and NGOs, working with stakeholders at every level, from grassroots organisations, regulators and industry partners to international networks. From circular systems design, including reduction, reuse and recycling solutions, to government relations and policy needs or social impact initiatives, I've managed international development projects and delivered community waste-collection pilots, national product stewardship initiatives and industry-driven plastic reduction programs, always with the emphasis on tangible outcomes and a genuine circular economy for people and planet.

As Kind Regards, I help organisations move from ESG commitments to measurable action, converting big targets into achievable steps, from planning to execution and reporting.
